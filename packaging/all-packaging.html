Get customers excited about your products with every box, pouch or bag they open.

Whether youre setting up your products on a shelf, mailing them directly to customers or wrapping them up for a gift, custom-branded packaging can make all the difference. Think of each custom package as its own promotional opportunity. Be sure to add your logo, business name and your company colors on each box, bag and mailer to drive brand awareness. We also offer a variety of packaging accessories to create a cohesive look. Hang tags, tape, wrapping paper and labels in your branding are a great finishing touch.

Found the right product for you? Great now its time to design. Youll first choose from a variety of sizes, shapes, materials and other options. Then choose from one of our templates or upload your own design. If you need more design help than that, weve got you. Contact one of our experts to get professional design help. Once your design is complete, well professionally print and ship your products to you.

Shipping packaging is made of durable B- or E-flute cardboard thats durable enough to be mailed. Product packaging is what you put your products in to help them stand out on a shelf or counter.

Many of our products have sample options. You can either place an order for 1 product with your design on it to get a pre-printed sample, or buy a pre-printed sample with a non-customized design. Our pre-printed samples give you an idea of the style and shape of our packaging. We also offer a sample kit for customers interested in seeing a variety of our custom pouches.

A great way to create a memorable unboxing experience is to have all your products match each other. When you create a cohesive look for all your packaging, boxes, labels and bags, it really creates that wow factor that customers will remember.
