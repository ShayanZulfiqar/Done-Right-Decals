As a Premium member, your individual customer homepage will display all ProAdvantage products available to you with the discount automatically applied. This discount is either up to 40% off, or the daily sitewide discount, whichever is greater.

We always ensure you benefit from the biggest discount available on the day of your order. For example, if there is a sitewide sale of 50% off, you receive this higher discount, but not a combination of sitewide discount plus ProAdvantage discount.

We can’t add a sitewide sale or product-category discount to your ProAdvantage discount (up to 40%), but we will apply the highest discount available on the day of your order. Your ProAdvantage discount cannot be combined with other offers or promotions that give you a discount on your total order.
