You know your audience best, and custom postcards from VistaPrint help you message them how, when and where you want. Send special offers in the mail, drop a thank you note into every package or add a coupon to the takeaways for your store counter or next event.

As the industry leader in small business marketing, we offer a huge assortment of options and designs to create just the custom postcards you want. You can get started by uploading your own design or browsing hundreds of professionally designed templates. Then, go with rounded corners for a modern look, turn some heads with embossed gloss or create a classic postcard using more traditional options. You can even add QR codes to make connecting online easier.

If you need help creating your custom postcards, our Design Services team is standing by. They can work with you to come up with a suite of matching products, including business cards, brochures, flyers and more. And if you’re strapped for time and resources, our mailing services option can make sending postcards easier than ever.

Frequently Asked Questions

Postcards are one of our most versatile products. Businesses have traditionally sent customers (or potential customers) postcards in the mail, and this is a great way to reach people in a way that’s convenient for them and cost-effective for you. However, there are a variety of other ways you can use custom postcards. If you have an office or retail location, you can use them as a handout. You can bring them to events as takeaways, or even add a coupon to your design and pass them out on the street like flyers. They also work well as packaging inserts when you’re shipping off products to customers, including thank you messages and instructions. With a wide range of sizes and designs, there’s no shortage of options for you.

Yes, there are a couple of ways to design your personalized postcard:

  • Customize one of our professionally designed postcard templates. You can narrow your search based on specific businesses and uses.
  • Start from scratch by picking one of the more basic designs from our library of templates. In the design studio, you can customize the shapes, text fields and colors for a completely unique design.
  • Choose the upload option to work with a print-ready postcard design.

Absolutely. Using photos on postcards is a great way to engage recipients, whether you’re showcasing a new house on the market, advertising a restaurant opening or announcing your annual sale. We recommend choosing a glossy finish to help your photos pop.

To add photos to postcards, select the image icon in our design studio or choose a template with pre-designated photo placements (you’ll be able to replace them with your own). Just be sure your photos have an image resolution of at least 300 dots per inch (DPI) and are saved in one of the following formats:
.jpg, .jpeg, .jfif, .bmp, .png, .gif, .heic, .svg, .pdf, .psd, .ai, .eps, .ait, .ppt, .pptx, .tif, .tiff
